Last updated: October 2025

Privacy First: E-Raksha is designed with your privacy and security as our top priorities. Your emergency data is sacred, and we handle it with the utmost care.

Data We Collect

Emergency Data

  • Location Information: GPS coordinates only during emergency activation
  • Emergency Events: Date, time, service called, and method of activation
  • Voice Commands: Processed locally on your device, not stored or transmitted

Personal Information

  • Emergency Contacts: Names and phone numbers (stored locally on your device)
  • Settings: Language preferences, enabled features
  • Device Information: Browser type and version for compatibility

How We Use Your Data

  • Emergency Response: Share your location with emergency services during active emergencies
  • Contact Notification: Send emergency alerts to your trusted contacts
  • App Improvement: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app reliability
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with emergency service requirements in India

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

  • Emergency contacts are stored locally on your device
  • Settings and preferences remain on your device
  • Emergency event history is kept locally (last 50 events)

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for emergency communications
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • No permanent storage of voice recordings
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data Sharing

We share your data only in these specific circumstances:

  • Emergency Services: Location and contact information during active emergencies
  • Your Emergency Contacts: Alerts with your location when you trigger SOS
  • Legal Requirements: If required by Indian law enforcement or courts

We NEVER sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Deletion: Request complete data removal
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Portability: Export your data in standard formats
  • Opt-out: Disable any non-essential data collection

Children's Privacy

E-Raksha can be used by minors under parental supervision. We encourage parents to:

  • Set up emergency contacts for their children
  • Review privacy settings regularly
  • Teach children about proper emergency app usage

Contact Us

For privacy concerns or data requests:

  • Email: privacy@eraksha.in
  • Address: E-Raksha Privacy Team, India
  • Response Time: Within 72 hours for emergency privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes through the app and this website.

Emergency Override: During active emergency situations, standard privacy restrictions may be overridden to ensure your safety and facilitate emergency response.
