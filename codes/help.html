‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does E-Raksha work without internet?
A: Yes! Core features like calling emergency numbers work offline. Location and contact notifications need internet.
Q: Can I use E-Raksha in different Indian states?
A: Yes! Emergency numbers (100, 101, 102, 112) work nationwide in India.
Q: How accurate is location sharing?
A: GPS accuracy is typically 3-5 meters outdoors, may be less accurate indoors.
Q: Will my emergency contacts know it's from E-Raksha?
A: Yes, the message clearly states it's from E-Raksha Emergency App.
Q: What if I accidentally trigger an emergency?
A: You have 10 seconds to cancel before the call is made. Press "Cancel" or say "Stop".
Q: Can children use E-Raksha?
A: Yes, with parental setup. Parents should configure emergency contacts and teach proper usage.

üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

Voice Recognition Not Working

  • Check microphone permissions in browser
  • Speak clearly and close to device
  • Try switching language in settings
  • Ensure stable internet connection

Location Not Detected

  • Allow location permissions when prompted
  • Enable GPS/location services on device
  • Go outdoors for better GPS signal
  • Wait 30-60 seconds for initial fix

App Not Loading

  • Check internet connection
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Update your browser to latest version
