üí° The Problem We Solve

In emergency situations, precious seconds can mean the difference between life and death. Traditional emergency response methods face several challenges:

  • Forgotten Numbers: People panic and forget emergency numbers
  • Complex Interfaces: Existing apps are too complicated during high-stress situations
  • Language Barriers: Limited multilingual support for India's diverse population
  • Location Communication: Difficulty explaining exact location to emergency services
  • Contact Notification: Family/friends remain unaware during emergencies
  • Accessibility: Limited support for elderly and differently-abled individuals

üë®‚Äçüíª Development Story

E-Raksha was born from a simple realization: in our increasingly connected world, accessing help during emergencies shouldn't be complicated.

After researching emergency response challenges in urban India and studying user behavior during high-stress situations, we identified the need for an intuitive, voice-first emergency solution.

Built using modern web technologies, E-Raksha leverages Progressive Web App capabilities to provide native app-like experiences while maintaining universal compatibility across devices and platforms.
